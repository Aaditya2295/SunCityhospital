About Ayushman Yojana

Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) is a flagship public health insurance scheme by the Government of India. Launched in September 2018, it aims to provide financial protection to over 50 crore economically vulnerable citizens by offering health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.

Objectives

  • Provide financial protection against catastrophic health expenses
  • Ensure access to quality healthcare services for all
  • Promote preventive healthcare measures
  • Reduce out-of-pocket healthcare spending

Benefits

  • Health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year
  • Coverage for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization expenses
  • Covers pre-existing conditions from day one
  • Portability of benefits across all states and Union Territories
  • Cashless and paperless transactions at public and empaneled private hospitals

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for Ayushman Yojana, families must fulfill the defined socio-economic criteria as per the latest Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) data. The scheme covers both rural and urban families based on predefined categories.

How to Avail Benefits

Beneficiaries can avail benefits under Ayushman Yojana by visiting the nearest empaneled hospital or Ayushman Mitra center. They need to carry their Aadhaar card or any other government-issued identification for identification and verification purposes.
