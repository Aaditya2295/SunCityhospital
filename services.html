Our Services

Outpatient Department (OPD)

The Outpatient Department provides medical consultations, diagnostic services, and treatment for patients who do not require overnight hospitalization. It offers various specialist clinics and is often the first point of contact for patients seeking medical care.

Surgical Department

The Surgical Department specializes in surgical procedures ranging from minor surgeries to complex operations. It is equipped with state-of-the-art operating rooms and staffed by skilled surgeons, anesthesiologists, and surgical nurses.

Inpatient Service (IP)

The Inpatient Service provides care for patients who require overnight hospitalization. It includes medical and surgical wards, intensive care units (ICUs), and other specialized units to monitor and manage patients' conditions round-the-clock.

Nursing Department

The Nursing Department comprises registered nurses and nursing assistants who provide patient care, administer medications, assist with procedures, and ensure patients' comfort and safety during their hospital stay.

Physical Medicine

The Physical Medicine Department focuses on rehabilitation and physical therapy services for patients recovering from injuries, surgeries, or medical conditions. It aims to improve mobility, strength, and overall functional abilities.

Paramedical Department

The Paramedical Department supports clinical services by providing diagnostic testing, therapeutic interventions, and other allied health services. It includes laboratory technicians, radiology technologists, and other specialized healthcare professionals.

Rehabilitation Department

The Rehabilitation Department offers comprehensive rehabilitation programs for patients with physical disabilities, neurological disorders, and chronic conditions. It includes physical therapists, occupational therapists, speech therapists, and other specialists.

Dietary Department

The Dietary Department provides nutrition assessment, counseling, and meal planning services for patients based on their medical conditions, dietary needs, and preferences. It ensures that patients receive appropriate nutrition to support their recovery and overall health.

Pharmacy Department

The Pharmacy Department dispenses medications, provides medication counseling, and ensures the safe and effective use of drugs for patients. It works closely with healthcare providers to optimize medication therapy and manage patients' medication regimens.

Operation Theater Complex (OT)

The Operation Theater Complex is equipped with advanced surgical facilities and equipment for performing surgical procedures. It includes operating rooms, pre-operative and post-operative areas, and support services to ensure smooth and safe surgical care for patients.

Emergency Room Services

The Emergency Room (ER) provides immediate medical care for patients with acute illnesses, injuries, or medical emergencies. It is staffed by emergency physicians, nurses, and other healthcare professionals trained to handle critical situations and stabilize patients.

Short-term Hospitalization

Short-term Hospitalization involves admission to the hospital for a brief period, typically less than 24 hours, for medical observation, diagnostic tests, or minor procedures that do not require overnight stays. It allows patients to receive specialized care and treatment on a short-term basis.

Blood Services

The Blood Services department manages blood donations, blood transfusions, and related services to support patients in need of blood products. It ensures the safe collection, testing, storage, and distribution of blood components to meet patients' transfusion requirements.

Laboratory Services

The Laboratory Services department conducts diagnostic tests, analyzes specimens, and provides accurate and timely results to assist in the diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment of various medical conditions. It includes clinical chemistry, hematology, microbiology, and other specialized laboratories.

Cardiology Services

The Cardiology Services department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of cardiovascular diseases and conditions. It offers a wide range of cardiac diagnostic tests, interventional procedures, and cardiac rehabilitation programs to improve heart health and outcomes for patients.

Orthopedic Services

The Orthopedic Services department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal disorders and injuries, including fractures, joint conditions, and sports-related injuries. It offers surgical and non-surgical interventions, rehabilitation programs, and orthopedic specialty clinics to restore mobility and function for patients.

NICU's, ICU's, Private Ward & General Ward

We also provide Neonatal Intensive Care Units (NICU), Intensive Care Units (ICU), Private Wards, and General Wards to cater to the specialized needs of patients requiring intensive medical care, personalized attention, or standard inpatient accommodations.
